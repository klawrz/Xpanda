# Privacy Policy for Xpanda

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

## Overview

Xpanda is a text expansion app for macOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your **email address** solely to authenticate you and manage your subscription. It is never used for advertising or marketing.

### Purchase History
We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect purchase and subscription data to process and verify your subscription status. See [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) for details.

### Text You Type
Text expansions and snippets you create are stored **locally on your device**. When you use the AI Rephrase feature, the text of your expansion is sent to a third-party AI provider (Anthropic or OpenAI) to generate a rephrased version. This is processed in real time and not stored by us. See [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy) and [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy) for details.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, payment information, location, contacts, or browsing history.
- We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.
- We do not sell your data to third parties.

## Data Storage

Account and subscription data is stored securely via Supabase. See [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for details.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: **support@baeside.app**

---

*This policy applies to the Xpanda macOS app.*
